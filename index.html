How I work

The strongest software teams scale through clear decisions, reliable operating models, and documentation that reduces ambiguity for everyone who joins later.

Design for change

I prefer small boundaries, readable modules, and explicit tradeoffs over clever abstractions that are hard to operate six months later.

Make quality visible

Tests, deployment notes, and architecture writeups are not polish-only extras. They are the public record of engineering judgment.

Raise team leverage

Good platform and backend work should lower cognitive load for the rest of the team by making the right path easier to follow.
